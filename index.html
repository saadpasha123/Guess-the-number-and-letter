WELCOME TO THE GUESS THE GAME

    Before starting the game, must read the following instructions.

  • Choose the game style: number guessing or letter guessing.
  • Select a difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  • If you choose number guessing, try to guess the correct number within the given range based on the difficulty level.
  • If you choose letter guessing, try to guess the correct letter from the alphabet.
  • For each correct guess, you will be told how many attempts it took to find the answer.
  • After you guess correctly, you can try to improve your score and beat your previous best!
  • Sign up to be on the Leaderboard and track your progress!
